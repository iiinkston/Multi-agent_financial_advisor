# Multi-Agent Financial Market Advisor

An AI-powered Multi-Agent Financial Advisory System that combines:

- Large Language Models (LLMs)
- Multi-Agent Collaboration
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Reinforcement Learning (PPO)
- Real-Time Financial Data Integration

to provide explainable investment recommendations across global financial markets.

---

## Key Features

### Multi-Agent Architecture

The system simulates a professional investment team:

- Fundamental Analyst
- Technical Analyst
- News Analyst
- Sentiment Analyst
- Risk Manager
- Trader Agent
- Quantitative PPO Agent

All agents collaborate through a Supervisor and Decision Fusion mechanism.

---

### Real-Time Market Analysis

Supports:

- US Stocks
- Hong Kong Stocks
- China A Shares
- Singapore Stocks
- UK Stocks
- Cryptocurrency Markets

via:

- Yahoo Finance
- Finnhub
- CryptoCompare

---

### Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Grounds reasoning using:

- Annual Reports
- Earnings Reports
- Macroeconomic Data
- Financial Research Documents

Reducing hallucinations and improving explainability.

---

### Quantitative Reinforcement Learning

Integrated PPO Agent:

- Learns market behaviour
- Generates quantitative trading signals
- Participates in final decision fusion
