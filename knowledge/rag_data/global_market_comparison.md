# Global Equity Market Comparison (2020–2025)

US market shows the highest annual return (~14.7%) with moderate-high volatility (~20.9%) and strong recovery after drawdowns.

UK market shows moderate return (~5.9%) with volatility around 16.6%, driven by cyclical sectors like energy and finance.

Singapore market has stable compounding (~7.0% annual return) with the lowest volatility (~14.2%), indicating defensive characteristics.

Hong Kong market has the highest volatility (~25%) and deepest drawdown (~-52%), reflecting policy and liquidity sensitivity.

China A-share market shows moderate return (~5.7%) with controlled drawdown (~-27%), influenced by government policy intervention.
